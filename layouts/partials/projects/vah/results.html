Results at a Glance

  1. Time on Task

    Relative to the automated voice system, we reduced time-on-task by nearly 400%

  2. Accessibility

    Differently abled users we tested with unanimously expressed pleasure with the experience, saying "I don't see how you could make it any easier” and “How easy is that... If it works like that... God, yeah, please.”

  3. Did It Ship?

    Just as we were about to launch, the 2022 Amazon layoffs happened—and the entire team working on regulatory medical voice applications was laid off, terminating a launch on consumer-facing Alexa smart speakers...

  4. Organizational Strategy

    ... but all wasn't lost—we built the groundwork for a comprehensive multimodal / conversational UI strategy that the VA could incorporate in building chatbots, IVRs, and voice-input in their mobile apps
